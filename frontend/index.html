The Studio

Every frame tells
a story

I'm Adak José — a photographer and filmmaker based in The Midlands, and the founder of Bigg Shots Media. I specialise in creating images and films that feel genuine rather than posed; the kind you'll return to years from now and feel exactly what you felt in that moment.

Behind every Bigg Shots session is a small, dedicated team — photographers, a videographer, and an editor — working together so nothing about your day goes uncaptured. Whatever the occasion, you get the same care, the same eye for detail, and one point of contact from first enquiry to final delivery.

Whether it's a wedding day, a growing family, a new arrival, a graduation, or a professional portrait — we bring the same patience and creative eye to every session.

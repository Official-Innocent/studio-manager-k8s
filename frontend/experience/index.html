Common Questions

What should I wear?
Wear something that makes you feel like yourself — comfortable and confident. If you have had something specially made for the occasion, wear it with pride — this is exactly the right moment for it.

For everyday outfits: avoid large logos or very busy patterns. Neutral or complementary colours work well for groups. Flowing fabrics photograph beautifully for maternity and portrait sessions.

If you are unsure, bring a change of outfit — variety gives you more options in the edit. I will include detailed outfit guidance tailored to your session type in your welcome email once you are booked.
What if I am nervous in front of the camera?
Almost everyone says this — and almost everyone forgets about the camera within five minutes. I will keep conversation going, guide your movement, and work with your natural energy. The best shots rarely come from posing — they come from a genuine laugh or a quiet in-between moment.
Can I bring someone with me?
Absolutely. Partners, children, and friends are always welcome. For Gold and Platinum packages, family and partners are included. For Onyx, additional people can usually be accommodated — just let me know when booking.
What happens if the weather is bad?
For outdoor sessions, I will monitor the forecast and be in touch 48 hours before. We can reschedule at no extra cost, move indoors, or embrace the moody weather — some of the best shots happen in overcast conditions when the light is soft and even.
How does the deposit work?
Your deposit secures your date and comes off your total. The remaining balance is due no later than 48 hours before your session — not after. This ensures both of us can focus entirely on the shoot itself, rather than any financial admin afterwards.

You will receive a balance invoice automatically once your deposit is confirmed, with clear payment instructions and a due date. Sessions will only proceed once full payment has been received.

Deposits are non-refundable if you cancel within 48 hours of your session, but can be transferred to a new date with reasonable notice.
How many photos will I receive?
This depends on your package tier — from 20 images on Onyx up to 50 on Platinum for session packages, and 80–300+ for wedding and commercial packages. You will receive at least the number stated. Every image is fully edited before delivery.
Do you travel outside Northamptonshire?
Yes. Travel within 30 miles of Rushden is included in all packages. Beyond that, a travel supplement applies based on distance — calculated at a standard mileage rate.

For longer distances, a minimum booking value may apply to ensure the journey is worthwhile for both of us. For example, a destination shoot in another county would typically require at least a Gold package. This is always discussed and agreed upfront — no surprises.

For destination weddings or shoots further afield, get in touch and we will work out a fair arrangement together.
